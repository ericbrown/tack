## The Problem

You return from a wildlife shoot with 2,000+ photos. Now you spend hours squinting at eyes, zooming to 100%, trying to find the keepers. Most shots are soft—motion blur, missed focus, blinking. Finding the 50 sharp ones takes forever.

## The Solution

Tack uses AI trained specifically on bird and wildlife eyes to automatically identify the sharpest images in your collection. What used to take hours now takes minutes.

## How It Works

1. **Select a folder** of photos from your shoot
2. **AI analyzes every image** for eye sharpness, detecting motion blur and focus quality
3. **Review the results** — sharp images on the left, rejects on the right
4. **Adjust the threshold** to keep more or fewer images
5. **Export to Lightroom** via XMP sidecar files with ratings and labels

## Features

- **Eye Detection AI** — Trained on thousands of bird and wildlife photos
- **Motion Blur Detection** — Automatically flags images with camera shake
- **Sharpness Scoring** — Quantitative ranking so the best rise to the top
- **RAW Support** — Works with CR2, CR3, NEF, ARW, RAF, DNG, ORF, RW2
- **Lightroom Integration** — Exports XMP files with star ratings and color labels
- **Blazing Fast** — Analyzes hundreds of images per minute on Apple Silicon
- **Works Offline** — All processing happens locally on your machine
